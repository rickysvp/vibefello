# VibeFello SEO + GEO Growth Playbook (English, List-First)

Last updated: 2026-04-15

## Objective

Grow qualified English waitlist signups first, then convert paid founder members.

## KPI Targets (first 60 days)

- Visitor to waitlist conversion: >= 4%
- Waitlist to paid conversion: >= 6%
- Organic pages indexed: >= 20
- Brand mentions in AI answers (GEO): steady week-over-week growth

## Strategy (Plan A)

1. Build authority around concrete launch blockers:
- Stripe checkout/webhooks
- Supabase production data flows
- Deployment and launch reliability

2. Publish problem-solving pages that LLMs can cite:
- Clear titles with technical specificity
- Step-by-step fixes
- Canonical URLs and structured metadata

3. Tight funnel linkage on every content page:
- One primary CTA to join waitlist
- Consistent messaging about priority support and launch outcomes

## Content Cadence (AI-assisted, 3-5 pieces/week)

Weekly asset mix:
- 2 troubleshooting guides
- 1 checklist/playbook
- 1 comparison page (optional)
- 1 case breakdown (optional)

Content template:
- Problem statement
- Why common AI answers fail
- Practical fix path
- Validation checklist
- CTA to waitlist

## GEO Layer (LLM discoverability)

- Maintain `/llms.txt` with high-value resource URLs.
- Keep topical guide pages crawlable and internally linked.
- Ensure concise factual summaries that are easy to quote.
- Update sitemap whenever new guide pages are added.

## Distribution Loop

- Publish guide pages to site first.
- Repurpose each page into X thread + Reddit post + short LinkedIn post.
- Link back to canonical guide URL to consolidate authority.

## Measurement Loop

Track these events:
- `page_view`
- `waitlist_submit`
- `checkout_start`
- `payment_success`

Review every week:
- Which pages drive highest waitlist conversion
- Which topics produce checkout starts
- Which sources produce paid members

Use findings to decide the next week’s 3-5 AI-generated pieces.

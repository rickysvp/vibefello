AI MVP Go-to-Market Checklist: From Launch to First Paid Users

This is a practical checklist for founders who already shipped an AI MVP and now need traffic and paid conversion without paid ads.

1) Build traffic from high-intent search pages

  • Publish problem-specific pages that match real user queries.
  • Use one page for one blocker and one call to action.

2) Turn proof into conversion fuel

  • Create short before/after case studies showing production fixes and outcomes.
  • Place proof near your payment CTA, not in a separate hidden page.

3) Shorten the path from intent to checkout

  • Ensure users can move from waitlist success to checkout with one clear next step.
  • Reduce optional choices in the critical conversion flow.

4) Track the only funnel metrics that matter

  • Monitor PV -> waitlist, waitlist -> checkout, and checkout -> paid.
  • Fix the largest drop first every week.

5) Keep trust signals real

  • Show real counts and real timeline updates.
  • Trust compounds when your data and messaging stay consistent.
